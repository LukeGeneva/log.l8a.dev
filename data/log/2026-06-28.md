title: Results That Don't Require Scrolling
---
The end-of-game panel was inline before — after you solved the puzzle you'd
finish on the board and then have to scroll down to see your stats. Swapped it
out for a fixed full-screen overlay with a blurred backdrop. The board stays
visible behind the modal.

Also moved the keypad to sit right next to the game board instead of below it.
The spatial relationship between the two makes more sense this way — you're
interacting with the board and the keypad together, so they should live together
visually.
