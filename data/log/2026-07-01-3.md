date: 2026-07-01
app: Crack'd
title: Shareable results
---
Crack'd's result modal now shows the emoji dot grid people are used to sharing
from these kinds of games, and the single share button became two: one that
copies the single-game result image, and one that copies a stats image with the
numbers and distribution bars baked in as a canvas render. Both fall back to
plain text if the clipboard image copy isn't supported.
