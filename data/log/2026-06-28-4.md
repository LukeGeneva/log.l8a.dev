app: RSS Feed Finder
title: Broader Than One Platform
---
Started as a YouTube RSS extension — YouTube doesn't surface RSS feeds in any
obvious way but they exist, and the extension would find them. Rebranded to RSS
Feed Finder because the underlying approach works on any webpage that includes
RSS discovery links, not just YouTube. The name was limiting what the tool
actually does.
