title: Padding That Fits the Platform
---
Instagram videos have a top navigation bar that overlays your content, which
means a clip that looks fine full-screen will have its first lines of code
hidden behind the nav on the actual platform. Added per-preset padding values so
the Instagram preset can define its own top and horizontal padding independently
of the global constants. The content shifts down and in just enough to stay
clear of the overlay.

Also fixed a bug where the first frame of every clip wasn't blank. The typing
animation is supposed to start empty and then type out the code, but char
timestamps were starting at frame 0 instead of frame 1, which meant frame 0
always had some content in it. One-off error, straightforward fix — timestamps
start at frame 1 now and the blank lead-in works correctly.
